John Ramsey

John B Ramsey is a two time New York Times best selling author, former U.S. Navy Lieutenant Commander, electrical engineer and entrepreneur. John led a private life focused on his family and business until Christmas 1996, when his youngest daugher JonBene't was brutally murdered in Boulder Colorado. He weathered years of injustice from the local police and international media that few have experienced with grace, dignity and God's care.

Through his writings and public speaking Ramsey shares his experiences and lessons with any who will listen in hopes that he can be an encouragement to those who face life's difficulties.
